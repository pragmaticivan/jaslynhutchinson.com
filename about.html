Hey there!

Welcome to the world of Jaslyn Hutchinson, where color and light collide and explode into phantasmagorical masterpieces!

… Or maybe this is merely an eclectic collection of paintings that have escaped from the musings of my brain. Whatever the case may be, I hope you enjoy the artwork that is being displayed here.

I have rivaling passions for oil and watercolors, and my inspiration for both was stemmed from my very talented mother and paternal grandmother. From the time I was six, I looked up to them and said I wanted to be an artist. I'm still painting away today and have plans to paint away the future as well, so…

Enjoy!

-Jaslyn Hutchinson
